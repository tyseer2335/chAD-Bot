A Novel Approach to Advertising

chAD Bot is a Discord Bot that allows companies to advertise their brand in your own server, but you also receive a portion of money for running ads on your server, creating a win-win situation for everyone.

Win-Win Situation

Users

chAD Bot users get to run the bot on their own servers. As the bot send Advertisement messages into the server, depending on a multiple factors such as frequency of advertisements and number of clicks, the server owner will be compensated with a monthly payment delivered to them just for keeping chAD bot in their servers.

Advertisers

Advertisers also benefit from our platform too since we are helping big and small companies gain more traction by running advertisements for them. Also helps ads reach out people with a wide range of interests on Discord.

Our Vision

With chAD, we plan to create the perfect opportunity for server-owners and marketers to achieve more with Discord servers. A perfect win-win scenario is created as marketers can promote their businesses on popular Discord servers while the owners of said servers get paid for using their platform. By adding the chAD bot to your server, for every thousand messages sent in a channel, the bot will send a message paid and designed by users wanting to promote their business. Each promoted message sent will pay the server owner. Take this golden opportunity to utilize one of the most innovative heavily used platforms in the world for your own gain.
